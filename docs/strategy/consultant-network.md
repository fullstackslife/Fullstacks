# FullStacks Consultant Network

## Purpose

The FullStacks Consultant Network exists to provide experienced hospitality operators, specialists, and recovery professionals who can support hotels, management companies, ownership groups, asset managers, and receivers during periods of operational pressure.

The network is not intended to function as a traditional staffing agency.

The objective is not volume.

The objective is operational capability.

FullStacks is building a network of qualified professionals who can help solve real operational problems inside hospitality environments.

---

# Core Philosophy

Quality over quantity.

Twenty qualified operators create more value than two hundred unvetted applicants.

The network should remain intentionally selective.

Every consultant should represent the FullStacks brand and be capable of operating inside active hospitality environments with limited supervision.

---

# Business Model

FullStacks may engage consultants in multiple ways depending on client requirements.

## Direct Placement

The client hires the consultant directly.

FullStacks may facilitate introductions, screening, coordination, or placement support.

Potential revenue:

* Placement fees
* Search fees
* Referral agreements

---

## Independent Contractor Model

The consultant operates as an independent contractor.

FullStacks contracts with the client and engages the consultant under a separate agreement.

Potential revenue:

* Assignment management
* Consultant margin
* Operational oversight

This is expected to be the primary model during early growth.

---

## Hybrid Model

Some engagements may begin under a contractor arrangement and later transition into direct employment relationships.

The appropriate structure should be determined by:

* client requirements
* legal considerations
* assignment duration
* consultant preference

---

# Employee Model

FullStacks does not currently intend to build a large W2 workforce.

The company should remain consultant-driven unless future growth clearly supports employee-based expansion.

The focus should remain:

* flexibility
* specialization
* operational expertise
* deployment speed

If W2 positions are added in the future, they should solve a specific business need rather than satisfy a growth target.

---

# Priority Roles

The consultant network should focus first on roles with the highest operational impact.

## Tier 1

### Task Force General Managers

The highest priority role.

These consultants provide:

* leadership coverage
* operational stabilization
* owner communication
* department coordination
* property recovery support

Task Force GMs represent the strongest alignment with the FullStacks mission.

---

### Executive Housekeepers

Housekeeping failures directly affect:

* inventory
* guest satisfaction
* inspections
* revenue

Strong housekeeping leadership is often one of the fastest ways to improve property performance.

---

### Revenue Specialists

Revenue visibility remains one of the most valuable capabilities FullStacks can provide.

Areas of focus:

* reporting
* AR visibility
* revenue integrity
* OTA reconciliation
* operational analysis

---

## Tier 2

### Maintenance Leaders

Maintenance impacts:

* inventory
* safety
* compliance
* recovery projects

These consultants become increasingly valuable during property recovery assignments.

---

### Operations Managers

Operations managers provide flexible support between department leadership and executive leadership functions.

---

# Geographic Strategy

The consultant network is intended to operate nationally.

The initial network will likely develop strongest in regions where FullStacks already has relationships and operational exposure.

Priority should be given to building density before building scale.

Ten trusted operators in one region create more value than one hundred unknown contacts scattered across the country.

---

# Consultant Standards

Every consultant should demonstrate:

* operational competence
* professionalism
* communication ability
* accountability
* hospitality experience
* problem-solving ability

Experience matters.

References matter.

Execution matters more than titles.

# FullStacks Value Proposition

Hotels do not hire FullStacks because they need a resume.

Hotels hire FullStacks because they need operational support.

The consultant network exists to expand the organization's ability to deliver solutions.

The objective is not simply matching people with jobs.

The objective is helping hospitality operators solve operational problems.

---

# Future Expansion

As the network grows, supporting systems may include:

* Consultant Availability Tracking
* Assignment Management
* Deployment Planning
* Travel Coordination
* Property Coverage Boards
* Performance Tracking

These systems should support operations rather than drive them.

People remain the primary asset.

Technology exists to make those people more effective.

---

# Guiding Principle

A smaller network of trusted operators is more valuable than a large database of unknown applicants.

Protect quality.

Protect credibility.

Protect the FullStacks reputation.
