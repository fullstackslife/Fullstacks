# FullStacks Business Model

## Vision

FullStacks exists to help hospitality operators regain control when properties, teams, reporting, systems, or leadership break down.

The long-term goal is to build a hospitality operations platform that combines experienced consultants, operational support systems, reporting tools, recovery workflows, and deployment infrastructure into a single organization.

FullStacks is not primarily a software company.

FullStacks is not primarily a staffing company.

FullStacks is not primarily a consulting company.

The long-term objective is to combine all three into a single hospitality operations platform.

Software supports operations.

Operations generate expertise.

Expertise drives consulting.

Consulting builds relationships.

Relationships create deployment opportunities.

The platform grows from operational credibility rather than technology marketing.

---

# Core Principle

We do not start with software.

We start with operational problems.

Every future product, workflow, dashboard, report, or system should originate from a real operational challenge experienced in active hospitality environments.

If a product does not solve a problem operators already pay to solve, it should not be prioritized.
