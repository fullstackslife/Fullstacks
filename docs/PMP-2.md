You are working in the FullStacks.ink codebase and helping define the first operational pilot program for future FullStacks hospitality products.

Context:

FullStacks is a hospitality operations, task force support, property recovery, reporting, and business systems company.

The objective is NOT to launch a public SaaS platform.

The objective is to create a pilot-ready operational toolset that can be tested in a real hospitality environment before broader commercialization.

Assume:

* Hotels already use PMS systems.
* Hotels already use payroll systems.
* Hotels already use accounting systems.
* Hotels already use communication systems.

FullStacks exists to solve operational visibility and execution problems that sit between those systems.

Do NOT propose replacing:

* PMS systems
* Payroll systems
* Accounting systems
* Existing hotel technology stacks

Instead, build around them.

---

## PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

Create a Pilot Program Framework that allows FullStacks to test operational tools in a real hospitality environment.

The pilot should be positioned as:

Operational Support Tools

NOT

Software Sales

The goal is to validate usefulness, workflow adoption, operational visibility improvements, and reporting effectiveness.

---

## PHASE 1 PILOT STRATEGY

Design the pilot around one property.

Focus on operational pain points already common in hospitality:

* Out-of-order rooms
* Vendor coordination
* Maintenance tracking
* Property recovery
* Reporting visibility
* Staffing visibility
* Task ownership
* Accountability
* Follow-up tracking

The pilot should help answer:

* Does this save management time?
* Does this improve visibility?
* Does this improve follow-through?
* Does this reduce spreadsheet dependency?
* Does this reduce information loss between departments?

---

## INITIAL PILOT MODULES

Prioritize only a small number of modules.

Recommended pilot candidates:

1. Recovery Board

Tracks:

* room number
* issue
* priority
* owner
* vendor
* estimated completion
* current status
* return-to-service readiness

Purpose:
Create operational visibility around OOO inventory and recovery efforts.

---

2. Vendor Tracker

Tracks:

* vendor
* project
* contact
* quote status
* scheduled date
* follow-up date
* completion status
* notes

Purpose:
Reduce vendor follow-up chaos and improve accountability.

---

3. Property Recovery Dashboard

Displays:

* OOO inventory
* active projects
* vendor status
* maintenance backlog
* high-priority issues
* recovery progress

Purpose:
Provide a management-level recovery view.

---

4. Weekly Owner Reporting

Generates structured reporting around:

* operational priorities
* major issues
* active projects
* recovery progress
* staffing concerns
* revenue visibility
* outstanding risks

Purpose:
Reduce manual report assembly.

---

## DO NOT BUILD YET

Do not build:

* Full SaaS subscriptions
* Billing systems
* Tenant management
* Enterprise permission systems
* Marketplace functionality
* Public product pricing
* Complex integrations

The objective is validation first.

---

## PILOT WORKFLOW

Design a workflow that supports:

Step 1:
Property enters issues.

Step 2:
Issues assigned to responsible party.

Step 3:
Vendors and maintenance tracked.

Step 4:
Progress updated.

Step 5:
Management reviews dashboard.

Step 6:
Owner reporting generated.

The workflow should feel operationally practical.

---

## PILOT DATA STRUCTURE

Propose data models needed for:

Recovery Items

Vendor Records

Projects

Owner Reports

Statuses

Assignments

Notes

Photos / Attachments (future-ready)

Keep schemas practical.

Avoid overengineering.

---

## PILOT SUCCESS METRICS

Create measurable pilot objectives.

Examples:

* Reduction in spreadsheet usage
* Faster issue visibility
* Improved vendor follow-up
* Improved room recovery tracking
* Better owner reporting consistency
* Reduced management reporting effort

Do not invent results.

Define what should be measured.

---

## PILOT PRESENTATION FRAMEWORK

Create internal-facing positioning language.

Use phrases such as:

* Operational Visibility
* Recovery Tracking
* Reporting Support
* Property Recovery Tools
* Operational Support Platform

Avoid phrases such as:

* AI-powered
* Disruptive technology
* Hotel operating system
* Revolutionary platform
* SaaS solution

The pilot should be framed as:

“Tools developed from real hospitality operational needs.”

---

## FUTURE EXPANSION PLANNING

After defining the pilot, identify how future modules could connect into:

FullStacks Recovery

FullStacks Operations

FullStacks Reporting

FullStacks Task Force

Without requiring redesign later.

## DELIVERABLES

Provide:

1. Recommended Phase 1 pilot architecture.
2. Recommended database structure.
3. Recommended workflows.
4. Recommended admin screens.
5. Recommended reporting outputs.
6. Success metrics to track.
